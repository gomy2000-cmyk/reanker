- **入れない要素**：人物、実在ロゴ、派手なアイコン。
- **禁止表現**：共通仕様に準拠。
- **生成プロンプト（英語・土台用）**：
  > Flat minimal vector illustration on off-white, three small cards in a row representing three meanings, each with a very simple icon (logo placeholder, gear, code brackets) and a tiny difficulty bar below. Calm navy and dark gray, 3 colors, generous spacing, no gradients, no 3D, no neon, no people. Leave blank space for Japanese labels.
